Reading Passage

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It was built in 1889 for the World's Fair. The tower stands approximately 330 meters (1,083 feet) tall and was the world's tallest man-made structure until 1930. Today, it remains one of the most recognizable and visited monuments in the world, attracting millions of tourists every year.
